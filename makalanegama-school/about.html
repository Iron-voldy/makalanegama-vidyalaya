Our Story

A Legacy of Educational Excellence

Makalanegama Vidyalaya stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the North Western Province of Sri Lanka. Located in the vibrant community of Galgamuwa, our school has been dedicated to providing quality Sinhala-medium education from Grade 1 to Grade 11.

Prime Location

Strategically located in Galgamuwa-Nikawewa Road, serving the educational needs of the local community with easy accessibility.

Co-Educational Environment

Fostering collaborative learning and diverse perspectives through our inclusive mixed-gender educational approach.

Provincial Type 2 Status

Recognized as a Provincial Type 2 school, maintaining high educational standards and government accreditation.
