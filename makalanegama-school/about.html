Our Story

A Legacy of Educational Excellence

Makalanegama Vidyalaya stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the North Western Province of Sri Lanka. Located in the vibrant community of Galgamuwa, our school has been dedicated to providing quality Sinhala-medium education from Grade 1 to Grade 11.

Prime Location

Strategically located at X8X5+VGH, Galgamuwa-Nikawewa Rd, serving the educational needs of the local community.

Co-Educational Environment

Fostering collaborative learning and diverse perspectives through our mixed-gender educational approach.

Provincial Type 2 Status

Recognized as a Provincial Type 2 school, maintaining high educational standards and government accreditation.

Our Heritage

A Rich Educational Heritage

Makalanegama Vidyalaya has been an integral part of the Galgamuwa community's educational landscape, serving generations of students with dedication and commitment to academic excellence.

1950s
Foundation Era

Established as a primary school to serve the educational needs of the local Galgamuwa community.

1980s
Expansion Period

Extended to include secondary education, becoming a complete school serving grades 1-11.

2000s
Modernization

Infrastructure improvements and introduction of modern teaching methodologies and facilities.

2020s
Digital Transformation

Launch of computer laboratory with support from Wire Academy & Technology for Village.
