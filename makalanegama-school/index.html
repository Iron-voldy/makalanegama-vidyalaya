About Our School

Building Tomorrow's Leaders

Makalanegama Vidyalaya, established in the heart of Galgamuwa, has been a cornerstone of education in the North Western Province. As a Provincial Type 2 school offering education from Grade 1-11, we are committed to providing quality Sinhala-medium education while embracing modern teaching methodologies.

Mixed-gender education fostering diverse perspectives and collaboration
