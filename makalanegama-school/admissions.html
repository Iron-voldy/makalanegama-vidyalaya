Admission Process

How to Apply

Our admission process is designed to be simple and supportive. Visit our administration office for personalized guidance throughout your application journey.

1

Visit Administration Office

Come to our school administration office during working hours (7:30 AM - 1:30 PM, Monday to Friday). Our friendly staff will welcome you and provide initial guidance about the admission process.

Personal Consultation Immediate Assistance
2

Collect Application Forms

Receive the official application forms and detailed information packet. Our administration team will explain all requirements, deadlines, and provide answers to your questions about our school programs.

Official Forms Detailed Guidelines
3

Submit Required Documents

Return to our office with completed application forms and all required documents. Our staff will review your submission and ensure everything is in order for processing.

Document Verification Application Review
4

Interview & Assessment

Attend a personal interview with school administration and teaching staff. For certain grades, a simple assessment may be conducted to understand your child's current academic level and learning needs.

Personal Interview Academic Assessment
5

Admission Confirmation

Receive admission decision and complete enrollment procedures at our administration office. Our team will guide you through orientation, fee payment, and preparation for your child's first day.

Enrollment Support Orientation Guidance

Required Documents

What to Bring

Please bring the following documents when visiting our administration office. Our staff will verify and guide you through the documentation process.

Student Documents

  • Birth Certificate (Original + Photocopy)
  • Previous School Records/Report Cards
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Medical Certificate/Vaccination Records
  • Recent Passport-size Photographs (4 copies)

Parent/Guardian Documents

  • National Identity Card (Original + Photocopy)
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill/Bank Statement)
  • Employment Certificate/Income Proof
  • Contact Information Form
  • Emergency Contact Details

Note: Our administration team will review all documents during your visit and may request additional documentation based on specific circumstances. Please bring original documents for verification along with photocopies.

Grade Information

Available Grades

We offer comprehensive education from Grade 1 to Grade 11, following the Sri Lankan national curriculum with modern teaching methodologies and dedicated teacher support.

Primary Section

Grades 1 - 5

Foundation building with focus on basic literacy, numeracy, and essential life skills. Our caring teachers provide individual attention to nurture young minds.

  • Play-based learning
  • Character development
  • Creative activities

Middle Section

Grades 6 - 9

Academic skill development with introduction to specialized subjects. Students develop critical thinking and prepare for advanced studies.

  • Subject specialization
  • Critical thinking
  • Practical learning

Senior Section

Grades 10 - 11

Preparation for O/L examinations with comprehensive subject coverage. Focus on academic excellence and future career guidance.

  • O/L preparation
  • Career guidance
  • Academic excellence

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our admission process. For more information, please visit our administration office.

Admissions typically open in November for the following academic year. However, we accept applications throughout the year based on availability. Please visit our administration office for current availability and specific grade openings.

Yes, there is a nominal application processing fee. The exact amount and payment methods will be explained by our administration staff during your visit. This fee covers application processing and assessment costs.

All applications must be submitted physically at our administration office. We believe in personal interaction to better understand your child's needs and provide appropriate guidance throughout the admission process.

Our primary medium of instruction is Sinhala, following the Sri Lankan national curriculum. We also provide strong support for English language development and offer Tamil language support when needed.

Transportation arrangements vary based on location and demand. Please discuss your transportation needs with our administration office during your visit, and we will provide information about available options in your area.

We offer various activities including sports, cultural programs, environmental projects, computer literacy programs, and traditional arts. Our administration team can provide detailed information about current programs and upcoming activities.

The complete admission process typically takes 2-3 weeks from application submission to final decision. This includes document verification, interview scheduling, assessment (if required), and final admission confirmation from our administration.
