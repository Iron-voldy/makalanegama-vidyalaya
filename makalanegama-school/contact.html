Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Find quick answers to the most commonly asked questions about our school.

Makalanegama School offers education from Grade 1 to Grade 11. We are a Provincial Type 2 school providing comprehensive education in Sinhala medium. Our curriculum follows the Sri Lankan national education standards and prepares students for O/L examinations.

School hours are Monday to Friday from 7:30 AM to 1:30 PM. The school office is open from 7:00 AM to 2:00 PM for administrative matters. We also offer after-school activities and tutoring sessions for interested students.

Admission applications can be obtained from the school office during office hours. For Grade 1 admissions, applications are typically accepted in February-March for the following academic year. Please bring necessary documents including birth certificate, immunization records, and previous school reports if applicable.

Currently, the school does not operate its own transport service. However, there are regular public bus services that connect to the school. Many families also organize private transport arrangements. For specific transport inquiries, please contact the school office.

Our school features modern facilities including a newly opened computer laboratory with 25 computers, science laboratory, library, sports grounds, and well-equipped classrooms. We also have ongoing environmental projects including gardening initiatives and sustainability programs.

As a government school, Makalanegama School follows the standard fee structure set by the Ministry of Education. Fees are minimal and cover basic administrative costs, development activities, and extracurricular programs. For detailed fee information, please contact the school office or visit during admission periods.
